# Ratios and Proportions

A ratio is a comparison of two quantities. A proportion is an equation stating that two ratios are equal.

## On the TEAS 7 Exam

Used frequently in dosage calculation questions. You must solve for X (e.g., if 5ml = 10mg, how many ml is 25mg?).
