Registering for the TEAS sounds simple, but choosing the wrong testing modality can cost you money and time.

## Option A: In-Person (PSI Testing Center)
* **Pros:** Stable internet, quiet environment, scratch paper provided.
* **Cons:** You must travel; rigid schedule.
* **Cost:** ~$115

## Option B: Remote (At-Home with Proctorio)
* **Pros:** Comfort of home; flexible start times.
* **Cons:** Strict tech requirements. If your internet blinks, your exam might be terminated.
* **Cost:** ~$115

## Step-by-Step Registration
1.  Go to the ATI Testing website.
2.  Create an account (or log in).
