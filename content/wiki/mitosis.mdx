# Mitosis

A part of the cell cycle when replicated chromosomes are separated into two new nuclei. Cell division gives rise to genetically identical cells in which the number of chromosomes is maintained.

## On the TEAS 7 Exam

The TEAS requires you to know the order of stages: Prophase, Metaphase, Anaphase, Telophase (PMAT).
