The ATI TEAS 7 (Test of Essential Academic Skills) is the gatekeeper for 80% of nursing programs in the US.

## What is a Good TEAS Score?

Most nursing programs require a minimum of 65%, but competitive programs often look for 78% or higher.

<GapCalculator />

## The 4 Sections of TEAS 7

1. **Reading:** 45 questions (55 minutes)
2. **Math:** 38 questions (57 minutes)
3. **Science:** 50 questions (60 minutes)
4. **English:** 37 questions (37 minutes)
